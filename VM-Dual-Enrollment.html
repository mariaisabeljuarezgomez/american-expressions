Getting Ahead

  • Vista partners with local community colleges to offer an array of courses for dual credit, meaning students will receive high school and college credit simultaneously upon successful completion of the course.

An Associate’s Degree while in High School

  • Vista provides students with the opportunity to progress toward and achieve an Associate’s Degree from its local community college partners through its articulated course pathways.

Transferable Credit

  • The dual enrollment courses that Vista offers in partnership with the local community colleges are transferable to most 4 year colleges and universities.

The College Experience

  • Most Vista dual enrollment courses are taught by a Community College Professor, preparing students for the rigors of college.
