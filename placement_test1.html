Welcome to Your English Assessment

This placement test is designed to assess your English proficiency level based on the foundational concepts from Lessons 1-10 of the "Inglés Idiomático" curriculum.

What This Test Covers:

  • Basic vocabulary and verb conjugations (to be, to have, etc.)
  • Articles (a, an, the) and adjective placement
  • Prepositions (on, at, in, from, to)
  • Simple present and present progressive tenses
  • Basic sentence structure and question formation
