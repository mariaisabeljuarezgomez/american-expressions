Beginner English Essentials (A1-A2) - Detailed Course Information

Build Your English Foundation with Confidence

Our Beginner English Essentials course is specifically designed for Spanish-speaking learners starting their English language journey. With comprehensive support and proven methodologies, you'll develop fundamental skills in reading, writing, listening, and speaking.

📚 Complete Course Curriculum

🎯 Course Overview

Duration: 40 weeks (September 2025 - June 2026)
Weekly Commitment: 4-6 hours (2 live sessions + self-study)
Class Size: Maximum 12 students for personalized attention
Language of Instruction: English with Spanish support as needed

📖 Module Breakdown

Module 1: Foundation & Introductions (Weeks 1-4)

  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Numbers, dates, and time
  • Personal information and family
  • Present tense of "to be"
  • Basic pronunciation patterns

Module 2: Daily Life & Routines (Weeks 5-8)

  • Daily activities and schedules
  • Present simple tense
  • Food, meals, and restaurants
  • Shopping and money
  • Frequency adverbs

Module 3: Places & Directions (Weeks 9-12)

  • Locations and directions
  • Prepositions of place
  • Transportation
  • Asking for help
  • Basic past tense introduction

Module 4: Leisure & Interests (Weeks 13-16)

  • Hobbies and interests
  • Sports and activities
  • Weather and seasons
  • Making plans and invitations
  • Modal verbs: can/can't

Module 5: Work & Study (Weeks 17-20)

  • Jobs and professions
  • Workplace vocabulary
  • School and education
  • Simple future with "going to"
  • Comparatives and superlatives

Module 6: Health & Emotions (Weeks 21-24)

  • Body parts and health
  • Emotions and feelings
  • At the doctor's office
  • Modal verbs: should/must
  • Present continuous tense

Module 7: Travel & Culture (Weeks 25-28)

  • Travel vocabulary
  • Countries and cultures
  • Hotel and accommodation
  • Past tense expansion
  • Cultural discussions

Module 8: Communication & Technology (Weeks 29-32)

  • Phone conversations
  • Email and messages
  • Technology vocabulary
  • Social media basics
  • Reported speech introduction

Module 9: Review & Real-World Practice (Weeks 33-36)

  • Comprehensive grammar review
  • Real-world scenarios
  • Interview practice
  • Presentation skills
  • A2 level preparation

Module 10: Assessment & Certification (Weeks 37-40)

  • Final project preparation
  • Speaking assessments
  • Writing portfolio review
  • Certificate ceremony
  • Next level preparation

📊 Assessment Methods

  • Weekly Quizzes (30%): Short vocabulary and grammar assessments
  • Monthly Projects (25%): Speaking and writing assignments
  • Participation (20%): Active engagement in live sessions
  • Midterm Assessment (10%): Comprehensive skills evaluation
  • Final Portfolio (15%): Collection of best work

🛠️ Learning Tools & Resources

  • Learning Management System: 24/7 access to materials
  • Interactive Workbook: Digital exercises with instant feedback
  • Video Library: Grammar explanations in Spanish and English
  • Pronunciation Tools: Speech recognition technology
  • Mobile App: Practice anywhere, anytime
  • Virtual Classroom: Live interactive sessions

🎓 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand and use familiar everyday expressions
  • Introduce yourself and others
  • Ask and answer personal questions
  • Interact in simple conversations
  • Write short, simple texts
  • Understand basic written information
  • Achieve CEFR A1-A2 proficiency level
