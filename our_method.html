The American Expressions Approach

Our methodology combines proven TESOL principles with innovative online learning technologies, specifically designed to address the unique challenges Spanish speakers face when learning English.

Under the expert guidance of Maria Isabel Juarez, M.A. TESOL, our institute has developed a comprehensive approach that accelerates English acquisition through structured, interactive, and culturally sensitive instruction.

Our Core Teaching Principles

Cognitive Language Learning

We engage multiple cognitive processes to enhance retention and understanding. Our lessons activate visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learning channels.

Communicative Approach

Real-world communication is our priority. Students practice English in authentic contexts, building confidence for actual conversations.

Scaffolded Learning

We build knowledge systematically, starting with foundational concepts and gradually introducing more complex structures. Each lesson builds upon previous learning.

Cultural Bridge Building

We respect and leverage students' Spanish language background, using contrastive analysis to highlight similarities and differences between Spanish and English.

Continuous Assessment

Regular formative assessments help track progress and identify areas for improvement. Our adaptive approach ensures targeted support.

Collaborative Learning

Students learn from each other through group activities, peer feedback, and collaborative projects that mirror real-world English usage.

Our 4-Stage Learning Process

Every lesson follows our proven four-stage methodology that maximizes retention and practical application.

1. Presentation

New language concepts are introduced through clear explanations, visual aids, and contextual examples. We connect new learning to students' existing Spanish knowledge.

2. Practice

Controlled practice activities allow students to use new language in structured exercises. Immediate feedback helps correct errors and reinforce correct usage.

3. Production

Students apply new language in creative, communicative tasks that simulate real-world situations. This builds confidence and fluency.

4. Performance

Students demonstrate mastery through presentations, projects, or assessments that showcase their ability to use English independently and effectively.

Technology-Enhanced Learning

Our state-of-the-art online platform integrates seamlessly with our teaching methodology to create an immersive, interactive learning environment.

Interactive Multimedia Lessons

Video, audio, and interactive exercises engage multiple senses for enhanced learning retention.

Real-Time Feedback

Instant correction and guidance help students learn from mistakes immediately.

Progress Tracking

Detailed analytics show learning progress and identify areas needing additional focus.

Virtual Classroom

Live video sessions with instructors provide personal interaction and immediate support.
