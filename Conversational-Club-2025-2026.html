Online Conversational English Club

Want to practice your English speaking skills in a relaxed, friendly, and supportive online environment? Join the American Expressions Conversational English Club! This club is perfect for Spanish-speaking learners of all levels (A2/Beginner+ recommended) who want to improve their fluency, pronunciation, and confidence when speaking English. Our moderated sessions focus on real-life topics, encouraging natural conversation and peer learning.

Club Details:

  • Meeting Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly sessions (choose your preferred frequency).
  • Session Duration: Typically 60-90 minutes per session.
  • Moderator: Facilitated by experienced AEI language coaches and native English speakers.
  • Format: Interactive online group sessions via Zoom, featuring themed discussions, informal debates, storytelling, and fun language games.
  • Group Size: Small groups (typically 6-10 participants) to ensure everyone gets a chance to speak.
  • Topics: Varied and engaging topics chosen by participants and moderators, including culture, hobbies, current events, travel, personal experiences, and more.

Why Join Our Conversational Club?

  • Boost Speaking Confidence: Practice in a non-judgmental space.
  • Improve Fluency: Get comfortable speaking more naturally and spontaneously.
  • Enhance Listening Skills: Understand different accents and speaking styles.
  • Expand Vocabulary: Learn new words and idioms in context.
  • Correct Pronunciation: Receive gentle guidance from moderators.
  • Connect with Peers: Meet other English learners from around the world.
  • Flexible & Fun: A great way to supplement formal courses or maintain your English level.

Who Can Join?

  • Anyone looking to improve their spoken English in a practical way.
  • Recommended for learners at A2 (Elementary) level and above. Beginners are welcome but may find it more challenging initially.
  • Students currently enrolled in other AEI courses, as well as new members.
  • You'll need a computer/tablet with a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone.

Membership & Schedule:

The Conversational Club operates on a subscription or drop-in basis. Detailed schedules with session times (accommodating various time zones) and topics will be available upon sign-up. We aim to offer sessions at different times throughout the week.

Sign-Up Process for 2025-2026:

Express your interest and get ready to chat! Sign-ups for the 2025-2026 club sessions are now open.

  1. Complete the simple sign-up form below to express your interest and preferred session times.
  2. Our Club Coordinator will contact you with available session schedules, membership options, and payment details.
  3. Choose your preferred sessions/membership plan.
  4. Receive your access links and join the conversation!

Membership Fees:

Affordable monthly subscription or pay-per-session options will be available. Please see our Club Fees page (link will be active soon) or inquire after sign-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main goal of the Conversational English Club?

The primary goal of the American Expressions Conversational English Club is to provide a relaxed, supportive, and engaging online environment where learners can practice speaking English regularly, improve their fluency, and build confidence in using the language for real-life communication.

It's less about formal grammar instruction and more about practical application, interaction with peers, and enjoying the process of becoming a more fluent English speaker.

Do I need to be enrolled in another AEI course to join the club?

No, you do not need to be enrolled in one of our formal courses to join the Conversational English Club. The club is open to all interested learners who meet the recommended minimum English level (A2/Beginner+).

It can be a great standalone option for those who primarily want speaking practice, or it can serve as an excellent supplement to our structured courses for extra conversational experience.

The American Expressions Institute Team

How are the topics for discussion chosen in the Conversation Club?

Topics for the Conversational English Club are chosen to be engaging, relevant, and suitable for a wide range of interests and English levels (within the club's scope).

Our moderators often propose themes based on current events, cultural interests, hobbies, travel, and everyday life scenarios. We also actively encourage club members to suggest topics they'd like to discuss. This collaborative approach ensures that the conversations are interesting and meaningful for everyone involved. A schedule of upcoming topics is usually provided in advance.
