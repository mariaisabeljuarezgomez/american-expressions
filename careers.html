Application Process

Our streamlined hiring process is designed to find the best educators who share our mission and values.

1

Submit Application

Send your resume, cover letter, and any required certifications to our HR team.

2

Initial Review

We'll review your application and reach out within 5-7 business days if you're a good fit.

3

Phone/Video Interview

A 30-45 minute conversation about your experience, teaching philosophy, and career goals.

4

Teaching Demonstration

Conduct a 15-20 minute sample lesson to showcase your teaching skills and style.

5

Final Interview

Meet with our leadership team to discuss the role, expectations, and next steps.

6

Welcome Aboard!

Complete onboarding, training, and start making a difference in students' lives.
