🎯 Perfect for Complete Beginners

Our Beginner English Essentials course is specifically designed for Spanish-speaking learners who are just starting their English journey. Whether you have no English knowledge or just basic phrases, this course will build your confidence from day one.

  • Reading simple texts
  • Writing short messages

Course Structure

Carefully designed modules to build your English step by step.

Modules 1-3: Foundations

Personal introductions, family, daily routines, basic grammar, and essential vocabulary for everyday situations.

  • Greetings & introductions
  • Present tense mastery
  • Numbers, time, dates
  • Family & personal info

Modules 4-6: Communication

Building conversation skills, asking questions, expressing preferences, and handling common social situations.

  • Food & shopping
  • Directions & places
  • Hobbies & interests
  • Making plans

Modules 7-10: Real World

Work, travel, health topics, technology use, and preparing for intermediate-level English communication.

  • Work & professions
  • Health & wellness
  • Travel & culture
  • Technology basics
