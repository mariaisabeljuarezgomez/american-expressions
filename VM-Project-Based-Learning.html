Interdisciplinary Learning

  • Because most situations in life do not happen in a silo, projects at Vista infuse elements of multiple content areas.

Real-World Connections

  • Tasks are authentic, and reflect the “real world.”

Core Learning

  • Projects are the hearts of units, rather than an additional task.

Structured Collaboration

  • Students must collaborate with others, building teamwork and other essential life skills.

Student Driven

  • Students, not teachers, direct the project.
