Your Complete Application Guide

Simple, Secure, and Fast Application Process

Apply for your English course in just 10 minutes! Our streamlined online process is designed with Spanish-speaking students in mind, with support available every step of the way.

üîç Step 1: Take Our Free Placement Test (Optional but Recommended)

Duration: 15-20 minutes
What it includes: Grammar, vocabulary, listening, and reading assessment

  • Determines your current English level
  • Helps us recommend the perfect course for you
  • Available in Spanish and English
  • No pressure - just helpful guidance!
Take Free Placement Test

üìù Step 2: Choose Your Course

Select the course that matches your goals and current level:

  • Beginner English Essentials (A1-A2): Perfect for complete beginners. Learn more.
  • Intermediate English Fluency (B1-B2): Build confidence in conversation. Learn more.
  • Advanced English Proficiency (C1-C2): Achieve near-native fluency. Learn more.
  • Business English & Communication: Professional English skills. Learn more.
  • TOEFL/IELTS Preparation: Exam preparation courses. Learn more.
  • Conversational English Club: Practice speaking in a relaxed environment. Learn more.

üìã Step 3: Complete the Online Application

Information you'll need:

  • Personal information (name, address, date of birth)
  • Contact details (phone, email)
  • Language background and learning goals
  • Previous English learning experience (if any)
  • Technical requirements confirmation

Time needed: 10-15 minutes
Language: Available in Spanish and English
Links to specific application forms are available on each course page or via the "Enroll Now" button.

üí¨ Step 4: Brief Placement Conversation (If Needed)

For some courses, we may schedule a short, informal conversation:

  • Duration: 10-15 minutes
  • Language: You can speak in Spanish or English
  • Purpose: Ensure you're placed in the right level
  • Format: Friendly video call or phone conversation
  • When: Within 2-3 business days of application

  • Receive email confirmation within 24 hours
  • Get detailed payment instructions
  • Access to student portal and course materials
  • Meeting link and schedule information
  • Welcome packet with technical setup guide

Prepare for your first class:

  • Complete technical check (camera, microphone, internet)
  • Join our student orientation session
  • Meet your instructor and classmates
  • Access your course materials and learning platform
  • Begin your English learning journey!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I apply if I speak no English at all?

A: Absolutely! Our Beginner course is designed for complete beginners. We provide support in Spanish to help you get started comfortably.

Q: How long does the application process take?

A: Most applications are processed within 24 hours. You'll receive confirmation and next steps via email.

Q: What if I'm not sure which level is right for me?

A: Take our free placement test, or speak with our admissions counselor. We'll help you find the perfect fit!

Q: Can I change courses after I start?

A: Yes! Within the first week, you can switch to a different level if needed. We want you to succeed!

Q: Do you offer classes in Spanish?

A: Our classes are taught in English to maximize your learning, but our instructors speak Spanish and can provide clarification when needed.
