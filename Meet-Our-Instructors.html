Professional TESOL-certified educators dedicated to your English learning success.

Maria Isabel Juarez, M.A. TESOL

Directora de Ingles Idiomatico English Institute

With over 15 years of experience in English language education, Maria Isabel Juarez leads Ingles Idiomatico English Institute with passion and expertise. Her Master's degree in TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages) from a prestigious university has equipped her with advanced pedagogical knowledge and innovative teaching methodologies.

Maria specializes in creating personalized learning experiences for Spanish-speaking students, understanding the unique challenges they face when acquiring English as a second language. Her approach combines traditional TESOL principles with modern technology to deliver engaging, effective online instruction.

Credentials & Expertise:

  • Master of Arts in TESOL
  • 15+ years of English language instruction experience
  • Specialized training in online education platforms
  • Expert in Spanish-to-English language acquisition
  • Certified in TOEFL and IELTS exam preparation
  • Advanced certification in Business English instruction
  • Fluent in Spanish and English
