Business English & Communication - Course Application for 2025-2026

This specialized course is designed for professionals and aspiring professionals who need to use English effectively in a business context. We focus on practical communication skills for the workplace, including presentations, meetings, negotiations, professional writing, and cross-cultural understanding. This course will equip Spanish-speaking learners with the language and confidence to succeed in the international business environment.

Course Details:

  • Start Date: October 6, 2025
  • End Date: May 29, 2026 (with options for extended modules)
  • Duration: Core modules approx. 30 weeks, 4-6 hours per week (plus industry-specific workshops)
  • Instructor: Experienced Business English trainers with corporate backgrounds. (e.g., Mr. Johnathan Smith, MBA, TEFL Certified)
  • Format: Fully online, utilizing case studies, role-playing, simulated business scenarios, guest speakers from various industries, and collaborative online projects via our LMS and Zoom.
  • Language Support: While the course is conducted in English, instructors are experienced in supporting Spanish-speaking professionals.
  • Materials: Digital access to leading Business English textbooks, industry-specific glossaries, authentic business articles, case studies, and templates for professional documents.

Learning Objectives:

  • Communicate confidently and appropriately in a variety of business settings (meetings, presentations, networking).
  • Write professional emails, reports, proposals, and other business correspondence effectively.
  • Participate actively and persuasively in negotiations and discussions.
  • Understand and use specialized business vocabulary and jargon relevant to your field.
  • Develop cross-cultural awareness for international business interactions.
  • Deliver clear, concise, and impactful presentations.

Course Outline (Example Modules):

  1. Module 1: Professional Introductions & Networking
  2. Module 2: Effective Business Meetings & Discussions
  3. Module 3: Business Correspondence: Emails, Memos, Reports
  4. Module 4: Presentation Skills & Public Speaking
  5. Module 5: Negotiation Strategies & Language
  6. Module 6: Cross-Cultural Communication in Business
  7. Module 7: Marketing & Sales Language
  8. Module 8: Finance & Accounting Terminology (Optional Specialization)
  9. Module 9: Human Resources & Management Communication (Optional Specialization)
  10. Module 10: Final Project: Business Plan Presentation or Case Study Analysis

Entry Requirements:

  • Minimum B1 (Intermediate) level of general English proficiency, confirmed by our placement test or prior AEI course completion.
  • Motivation to apply English skills in a professional context.
  • Access to a computer/tablet with stable internet, webcam, and microphone.

Certification:

Upon successful completion, students receive a Certificate in Business English & Communication from American Expressions Institute. Options for specialized endorsements (e.g., Business English for Marketing) may be available.

Enrollment Process for 2025-2026:

Applications for the 2025-2026 Business English & Communication course are open.

  1. Complete the online application form (button below).
  2. Take our online placement test to assess your current English level for business contexts.
  3. You may be invited for a brief online conversation to discuss your professional goals and suitability for the course.
  4. Receive your admission offer and details for tuition payment.
  5. Gain access to course materials and start enhancing your professional English!

This course is popular; early application is recommended to secure your place.

What kind of industries or professions is this Business English course most suitable for?

Our Business English & Communication course is designed to benefit professionals across a wide range of industries. Core modules cover universally applicable skills such as meetings, presentations, and professional writing.

Additionally, we offer optional specialization modules that delve into language specific to sectors like:

  • Marketing and Sales
  • Finance and Accounting
  • Human Resources and Management
  • IT and Technology
  • International Trade and Logistics

During the application process, you can indicate your industry or area of interest, which helps us tailor certain case studies and examples.

Is there a focus on understanding different business cultures?

Yes, absolutely. A significant component of our Business English course is "Cross-Cultural Communication in Business." We understand that effective international business requires more than just language proficiency; it demands cultural sensitivity and awareness.

This module covers topics such as:

  • Understanding different communication styles (direct vs. indirect).
  • Business etiquette in various cultures.
  • Negotiation tactics across cultures.
  • Building international professional relationships.
