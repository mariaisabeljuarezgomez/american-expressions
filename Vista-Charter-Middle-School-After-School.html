Field Trips

Field Trips are an integral part of our after school program.  We believe in creating lifelong memories for our students with their friends.  Our after school and break field trip include places such as:

  • Disneyland
  • Knott’s Berry Farm
  • Magic Mountain
  • LA Zoo
  • Medieval Times
  • Hiking
  • Paramount Studio Tours

Athletics

Vista continually shows what it is to have a Championship winning sports program.  Sports teams include:

  • Flag Football
  • Basketball
  • Soccer
  • Volleyball

School Clubs

Vista believes in enriching our students' lives through after school clubs.  These clubs expose students to activities and events that they may not experience elsewhere.  Clubs include:

  • Rock Climbing
  • Chess
  • Cooking
  • Digital Media
  • Japanese Cultural Club
  • Rock Band

Tutoring

Every student who attends our after school program gets individual homework help and tutoring from our trained support staff.  Students are incentivized to take ownership of their learning and learn how to advocate for themselves.
