Advanced English Proficiency (C1-C2)

 

Advanced English Proficiency (C1-C2) - Course Application for 2025-2026

This course is designed for highly proficient English learners aiming to achieve near-native fluency and master nuanced communication. You will refine your ability to understand a wide range of demanding, longer texts, and recognize implicit meaning. You'll learn to express yourself fluently and spontaneously without much obvious searching for expressions, using language flexibly and effectively for social, academic, and professional purposes. Our challenging online curriculum is tailored for Spanish speakers ready to perfect their English.

Course Details:

  • Start Date: September 22, 2025
  • End Date: June 26, 2026
  • Duration: Approximately 40 weeks, 6-8 hours per week (including advanced seminars, independent research, and peer collaboration)
  • Instructor: Senior ESL instructors with expertise in advanced language acquisition. (e.g., Dr. Laura Chen, PhD Linguistics)
  • Format: Fully online, incorporating advanced academic seminars, debate sessions, critical analysis of complex texts, presentation skills workshops, and peer-led discussions via our LMS and Zoom.
  • Language Support: Instruction and all materials are exclusively in English to foster full immersion at this advanced level.
  • Materials: Access to academic journals, contemporary literature, advanced grammar and style guides, and specialized online research databases.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand with ease virtually everything heard or read.
  • Summarize information from different spoken and written sources, reconstructing arguments and accounts in a coherent presentation.
  • Express yourself spontaneously, very fluently and precisely, differentiating finer shades of meaning even in more complex situations.
  • Produce clear, well-structured, detailed text on complex subjects, showing controlled use of organizational patterns, connectors and cohesive devices.
  • Engage in sophisticated discussions, debates, and presentations with confidence.
  • Analyze and critique complex written and spoken English from various genres.

Course Outline (Example Modules):

  1. Module 1: Advanced Academic Discourse & Critical Reading
  2. Module 2: Persuasive Writing & Argumentation
  3. Module 3: Understanding Idiomatic & Colloquial English
  4. Module 4: Public Speaking & Professional Presentations
  5. Module 5: Analysis of Literary & Non-Literary Texts
  6. Module 6: Cross-Cultural Communication in Professional Contexts
  7. Module 7: Media Literacy & Analysis of Current Affairs
  8. Module 8: Advanced Research & Report Writing
  9. Module 9: Nuances of English Pronunciation & Prosody
  10. Module 10: Capstone Project & Portfolio Development

Entry Requirements:

  • Completion of an Upper-Intermediate (B2) level English course OR equivalent high proficiency demonstrated through our comprehensive online placement test and interview.
  • Strong ability to understand and produce complex English.
  • Commitment to rigorous academic study and active participation.
  • Reliable computer, stable internet, webcam, and microphone.

Certification:

Upon successful completion, students receive a Certificate of Advanced Proficiency from American Expressions Institute, indicating mastery at the CEFR C1/C2 level.

Enrollment Process for 2025-2026:

Applications for the 2025-2026 Advanced English Proficiency course are now being accepted.

  1. Submit the online application form (button below).
  2. Complete our advanced online placement test and schedule a brief interview with an instructor.
  3. Receive notification of your application status and placement.
  4. Finalize enrollment with tuition payment and receive your course schedule.
  5. Begin your journey to English mastery with American Expressions Institute!

Due to the intensive nature of this course, enrollment is selective. Early application is highly recommended.

What types of authentic materials are used in the Advanced course?

Our Advanced English Proficiency course utilizes a diverse range of authentic materials to expose you to real-world English, including:

  • Contemporary news articles from reputable sources (e.g., The New York Times, BBC News).
  • Academic essays and research papers on various subjects.
  • Excerpts from modern literature, plays, and poetry.
  • Documentaries, TED Talks, and university lectures.
  • Podcasts and interviews on current affairs and specialized topics.
  • Professional reports and case studies.

This approach helps you develop the skills to understand and engage with English as it is used by native speakers in academic, professional, and social contexts.

Will this course prepare me for standardized tests like TOEFL or IELTS at an advanced level?

While this Advanced English Proficiency course is not solely a test preparation course, the high-level skills you develop in reading, writing, listening, and speaking are directly applicable to achieving high scores on exams like TOEFL iBT and IELTS Academic.

You will practice critical analysis, synthesizing information, constructing complex arguments, and understanding nuanced language – all essential for these exams. For dedicated test-taking strategies and practice, we also offer a separate Exam Preparation course.

What is the Capstone Project mentioned in the Advanced course?

The Capstone Project is a culminating assignment in the Advanced English Proficiency course where you will apply the comprehensive skills learned throughout the program.

It typically involves in-depth research on a topic of your choice, culminating in a substantial written report or academic paper, and an oral presentation delivered to your peers and instructors. This project allows you to demonstrate your ability to use English effectively for complex academic or professional purposes, showcasing your research, analytical, writing, and presentation skills at a C1/C2 level.
