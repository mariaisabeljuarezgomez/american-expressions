Intermediate English Fluency

Build Confidence in Conversation. Take your English to the next level with our comprehensive intermediate program (B1-B2 CEFR).

Course Overview

From basic communication to confident conversation

🎯 Perfect for Growing Learners

Our Intermediate English Fluency course is designed for Spanish-speaking learners who have basic English knowledge and want to become truly conversational. If you can handle simple situations but struggle with complex topics, this course is for you.

  • Writing essays and reports

Course Structure

Progressive modules to develop fluency and confidence

Modules 1-3: Grammar Mastery

Advanced tenses, conditionals, passive voice, and complex sentence structures for sophisticated communication.

  • Perfect tenses mastery
  • Conditional sentences
  • Passive voice usage
  • Advanced modals

Modules 4-6: Cultural Fluency

Understanding cultural contexts, idioms, colloquialisms, and natural speech patterns of native speakers.

  • Idioms & expressions
  • Cultural nuances
  • Informal language
  • Regional differences

Modules 7-9: Academic & Professional

Workplace communication, academic writing, presentations, and preparing for advanced English proficiency.

  • Business communication
  • Academic writing
  • Presentation skills
  • Critical thinking

Advanced Learning Experience

Immerse yourself in authentic English communication with real-world applications.

Discussion & Debate Sessions

Engage in meaningful conversations about current events, social issues, and topics that matter to you. Build confidence expressing complex ideas.

Authentic Media Analysis

Work with real podcasts, news articles, and videos to develop listening skills and learn how native speakers actually communicate.

Presentation & Speaking Practice

Deliver presentations, participate in role-plays, and practice job interview scenarios to build professional confidence.
