After School Program

Vista Condor partners with Think Together to enhance student learning for students across all grade levels, after school. 
 
Students enrolled in the program experience:
  • small class sizes
  • learning centered on STEAM based approach that provides students with opportunities to increase problem solving skills and engage in real world thinking
  • youth development
  • art and enrichment centered activities

Athletics

Vista Condor provides opportunities to develop skills within various sports while promoting team building and positive interactions among peers. Vista Condor partners with Active Education which strives to encompass fun and healthy activities with an emphasis on Social Emotional Learning and Positive Behavioral Intervention Supports (PBIS). All students are provided opportunities to develop their talents and abilities both during the school day and afterschool. 

School Clubs

Vista Condor provides opportunities for students to further develop their talents and abilities through participation in various school clubs including:
 
  • Student Council Leadership Team
  • Drama
  • Art 
  • Science
  • Girl's Scouts
  • Cheerleading
  • basketball
  • Rock Band
  • Yearbook

Tutoring

After School tutoring opportunities are provided to all students in reading and mathematics throughout the school year. Parents and families are also welcomed to participate in its Latino Literacy program which aims to promote language acquisition and reading comprehension.
