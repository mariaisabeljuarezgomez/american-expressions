📊 Interactive English Level Assessment

🎯 What This Test Will Tell You:

  • Your current English proficiency level (A1-C2)
  • Recommended course for optimal learning
  • Specific skills to focus on improving
  • Personalized learning path suggestions

📋 Test Information:

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes
  • Questions: 20 carefully selected items
  • Skills Tested: Grammar, vocabulary, reading, listening
  • Format: Multiple choice + audio questions
  • Language Support: Instructions available in Spanish

✅ Before You Start:

  • Find a quiet place where you can concentrate
  • Make sure your audio is working (you'll hear some questions)
  • Answer honestly - this helps us help you!
  • Don't worry about perfect scores - this is just to find your starting point
