Mission

Our mission is to create a transformative English learning experience that is engaging, culturally responsive, and builds a strong communication foundation for academic and career success. We aim to provide quality English instruction for Spanish-speaking students by focusing on both linguistic competence and cultural confidence as critical tools of student engagement in learning. Our supportive community of dedicated educators focuses on developing students' English language skills, as well as their communication strategies, cultural awareness, and confidence for success in English-speaking academic and professional environments.

Vision

Our vision is to bridge the communication gap for Spanish-speaking students seeking to master English as their pathway to academic, professional, and personal success. American Expressions Institute students develop global communication competence through comprehensive online English instruction; they are able to express themselves confidently in academic and professional settings, conduct effective research in English, recognize cultural nuances in communication, articulate their ideas clearly and persuasively, and take action to achieve their educational and career goals. Students have a voice in their learning journey, applying English language skills to real-world scenarios that have personal and professional significance, with numerous opportunities to demonstrate their mastery to authentic audiences. Most importantly, American Expressions Institute envisions empowering students who enter to learn English but leave ready to confidently navigate English-speaking environments and achieve their dreams through effective communication.

Educational Philosophy

At American Expressions Institute, we are committed to transforming the English learning experience for Spanish-speaking students. From the moment you join our online community, you will notice the difference. Beyond our welcoming virtual environment is a team of highly qualified TESOL-certified instructors, academic coordinators, and student support specialists, all of whom share the goal of ensuring that our students are highly engaged in their English learning journey and feel 100% supported as part of the American Expressions family. American Expressions Institute is the right fit for English learners of all proficiency levels and aspirations, with an essential commitment to personalized instruction and a focus on improving academic, professional, and social communication skills in a global context.

Learning Environment

At American Expressions Institute, we believe engaging online classrooms are successful classrooms. The virtual learning environment is a major key to instructor and student satisfaction, and it is the setting in which a culture of confident English communication occurs. The learning environment at American Expressions Institute takes into account the whole student and incorporates important aspects of the digital platform, classroom interaction norms and values, the role of communicative pedagogy and student-centered instructional approaches, positive reinforcement strategies, and family support engagement.
