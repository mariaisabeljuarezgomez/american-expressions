Beginner English Course Application (2025-2026)

Ready to start your English learning journey? Apply for our Beginner English course designed for Spanish speakers.

Home > Enroll > Beginner English Application

Apply for Beginner English Essentials (A1-A2): 2025-2026

This course is designed for absolute beginners or those with very limited English knowledge who want to start communicating effectively in English. By the end of this course, you will understand basic grammar, essential vocabulary, and be able to engage in simple conversations for everyday situations. Our online platform offers interactive lessons and support tailored for Spanish-speaking learners.

Course Details:

  • Start Date: September 8, 2025
  • End Date: June 12, 2026
  • Duration: Approximately 40 weeks, 4-6 hours per week (including live sessions and self-study)
  • Instructor: Led by certified ESL instructors with experience teaching Spanish speakers: Maria Isabel Juarez, M.A. TESOL, Directora de Ingles Idiomatico English Institute, TESOL Certified)
  • Format: Fully online, combining live interactive Zoom sessions, pre-recorded video lessons, and exercises on our Learning Management System (LMS).
  • Language Support: Course instructions, glossaries, and initial support available in Spanish. Spanish-speaking teaching assistants available during certain hours.
  • Materials: All digital materials, including e-textbooks, PDF worksheets, interactive quizzes, and access to our online resource library, are included.

Learning Objectives:

  • Introduce yourself and others, ask and answer basic personal questions.
  • Understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases.
  • Interact in a simple way provided the other person talks slowly and clearly.
  • Understand and use basic English grammar (e.g., simple present tense, common nouns, adjectives).
  • Build a foundational vocabulary of over 500 common English words.
  • Develop basic listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills for CEFR A1-A2 levels.

Course Outline (Example Modules):

  1. Module 1: Greetings & Introductions
  2. Module 2: Numbers, Alphabet, and Telling Time
  3. Module 3: Everyday Objects & People
  4. Module 4: Basic Verbs & Simple Sentences
  5. Module 5: Talking About Family & Friends
  6. Module 6: Food & Drinks
  7. Module 7: Places in the City & Directions
  8. Module 8: Hobbies & Daily Routines
  9. Module 9: Simple Past & Future Expressions
  10. Module 10: Review & Next Steps

Entry Requirements:

  • No prior English knowledge is required.
  • A short, informal placement conversation (in Spanish or English) may be conducted to ensure this is the right level for you.
  • Access to a computer or tablet with a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone.

Certification:

Upon successful completion of the course and assessments, students will receive a Certificate of Completion from Ingles Idiomatico English Institute, indicating proficiency at the CEFR A1/A2 level.

Enrollment Process for 2025-2026:

Applications for the 2025-2026 academic year are now open!

  1. Complete and submit the online application form (see button below).
  2. Our admissions team will contact you to schedule a brief placement chat if necessary.
  3. Receive your acceptance and information on tuition payment.
  4. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive access to the course materials and schedule.

We enroll students on a first-come, first-served basis until course capacity is met. Early application is encouraged!

Tuition:

Please visit our Tuition and Fees page for detailed information on course costs and payment options. Early bird discounts are available until July 31, 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the technical requirements for the online course?

To participate in our online courses, you will need:

  • A reliable computer (desktop or laptop), tablet, or smartphone.
  • A stable internet connection (broadband recommended for live sessions).
  • A working webcam and microphone for interactive live sessions.
  • An up-to-date web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  • Ability to view PDF documents and play video/audio content.

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly. If you have specific concerns, please contact our tech support at support@inglesidiomatico.com.

Is this "Beginner English Essentials" course right for me if I know no English at all?

Absolutely! This course is specifically designed for individuals with little to no prior English knowledge. We start with the very basics, such as the alphabet, greetings, and numbers.

Our curriculum gradually builds your skills in listening, speaking, reading, and writing. We provide ample support, including materials and guidance in Spanish, to ensure you feel comfortable and confident as you begin your English learning journey.

Ingles Idiomatico English Institute Team

How is my progress assessed in the online course?

Your progress is assessed through a variety of methods to ensure a comprehensive understanding of your development:

  • Regular Quizzes: Short quizzes after lessons to check understanding of new vocabulary and grammar.
  • Assignments: Practical tasks including short writing exercises, speaking recordings, and interactive activities.
  • Participation: Active engagement in live online sessions and discussion forums.
  • Module Tests: Comprehensive tests at the end of each major module covering all skills.
  • Final Assessment: A final evaluation to measure overall proficiency gained throughout the course.

Instructors provide regular feedback to help you identify areas for improvement and track your learning.
