TOEFL/IELTS Exam Preparation - Course Application for 2025-2026

This intensive course is specifically designed to help Spanish-speaking students achieve their target scores on the TOEFL iBT or IELTS (Academic/General Training) exams. We provide comprehensive coverage of all test sections, focusing on essential strategies, time-management skills, and extensive practice with authentic test materials. Our expert instructors will guide you through the nuances of each exam, helping you build the confidence and skills needed for success.

Course Details:

  • Flexible Start Dates: New cohorts begin monthly (e.g., First Monday of each month, 2025-2026).
  • Duration: Typically 8-12 weeks per module (TOEFL or IELTS specific), 10-15 hours per week (including live strategy sessions, practice tests, and individual review).
  • Instructor: Certified exam preparation specialists with extensive experience in TOEFL/IELTS training. (e.g., Ms. Sarah Miller, MA Applied Linguistics, Certified Examiner).
  • Format: Fully online, combining live interactive strategy workshops, timed practice sections, full-length mock exams, and personalized feedback on speaking and writing tasks via our LMS and Zoom.
  • Language Support: While exam strategies are taught in English, instructors can provide clarifications in Spanish for complex concepts if needed by learners.
  • Materials: Access to official TOEFL/IELTS preparation guides, extensive banks of practice questions, proprietary strategy workbooks, and online simulation tools.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the structure and question types for all sections of the chosen exam (TOEFL iBT or IELTS).
  • Master effective test-taking strategies for reading, listening, speaking, and writing sections.
  • Improve time-management skills for each section of the exam.
  • Develop academic English skills required for higher scores (e.g., note-taking, summarizing, paraphrasing, synthesizing information).
  • Practice extensively with authentic exam questions and receive detailed feedback.
  • Build confidence and reduce test anxiety through familiarity and practice.

Course Outline (Example for TOEFL iBT):

  1. Module 1: Introduction to TOEFL iBT & Diagnostic Test
  2. Module 2: Reading Section Strategies & Practice
  3. Module 3: Listening Section Strategies & Practice
  4. Module 4: Speaking Section - Integrated & Independent Tasks Strategies & Practice
  5. Module 5: Writing Section - Integrated & Independent Tasks Strategies & Practice
  6. Module 6: Vocabulary & Grammar for Academic English
  7. Module 7: Time Management & Pacing Strategies
  8. Module 8: Full-Length Mock Tests & Performance Analysis
  9. (Similar structure would apply for IELTS specific course)

Entry Requirements:

  • Minimum B1+ (Upper-Intermediate) level of general English proficiency recommended, confirmed by our placement test.
  • Clear goal of taking the TOEFL or IELTS exam for academic, immigration, or professional purposes.
  • Access to a computer/tablet with stable internet, webcam, and microphone (especially for speaking practice).

Certification/Outcome:

While this course prepares you for the official exam, American Expressions Institute provides a Certificate of Completion for the preparation course. The primary outcome is readiness to achieve your target score on the official TOEFL or IELTS test.

Enrollment Process for 2025-2026:

Prepare for your exam success! Applications for 2025-2026 are ongoing.

  1. Submit the online application form (button below), indicating your target exam (TOEFL or IELTS) and preferred start month.
  2. Take our English placement test to ensure the course is a good fit for your current level.
  3. Our advisors will contact you to discuss your test goals and confirm your enrollment.
  4. Complete tuition payment to secure your spot.
  5. Receive access to all course materials and begin your focused preparation!

Which exam does this course prepare for, TOEFL or IELTS, or both?

Our Exam Preparation program offers dedicated courses for both TOEFL iBT and IELTS (Academic and General Training modules). When you apply, you will specify which exam you are preparing for.

Each course is tailored to the specific format, question types, scoring criteria, and strategies unique to that particular exam. While some underlying English skills are common, the test-specific preparation is distinct for each.

What is the recommended English level to start an Exam Preparation course?

To benefit most from our TOEFL or IELTS preparation courses, we recommend students have at least a strong B1 (Intermediate) or preferably a B2 (Upper-Intermediate) level of general English proficiency.

These exams test academic English skills, so a solid foundation in grammar, vocabulary, and comprehension is crucial before focusing on test-specific strategies. If you are unsure of your level, our placement test will help determine your readiness. We may recommend one of our general English courses first if your level is below B1.

The American Expressions Institute Academic Team

How many full-length practice tests are included in the course?

Our Exam Preparation courses typically include 2 to 3 full-length, timed mock exams that simulate the actual test environment. The exact number can vary slightly depending on the specific course duration and intensity.

In addition to full tests, you will have extensive practice with individual sections and question types throughout the course. Detailed feedback and performance analysis are provided for all practice tests to help you identify areas for improvement.
