General Information

What is Ingles Idiomatico English Institute?

Ingles Idiomatico English Institute is a specialized English language school focused on helping Spanish-speaking learners achieve fluency in English. We offer comprehensive courses from beginner to advanced levels, including specialized programs for business English, test preparation, and conversational practice.

Where is Ingles Idiomatico English Institute located?

We are located in El Paso, Texas, at 123 Language Learners Lane. However, all our courses are conducted online, so you can join from anywhere with an internet connection.

Do you offer both online and in-person classes?

Currently, all our courses are conducted online via interactive video conferencing platforms. This allows us to serve students from around the world and provide flexible scheduling options.

Courses & Programs

What English courses do you offer?

We offer six main programs:

  • Beginner English Essentials (A1-A2) - Foundation skills
  • Intermediate English Fluency (B1-B2) - Building confidence
  • Advanced English Proficiency (C1-C2) - Near-native fluency
  • Business English & Communication - Professional skills
  • TOEFL/IELTS Preparation - Test preparation
  • Conversational English Club - Practice speaking
How do I know which level is right for me?

We offer a free online placement test that assesses your current English level. This test evaluates your grammar, vocabulary, reading, and listening skills to recommend the most appropriate course for you.

How long are the courses?

Course durations vary by program:

  • General English courses (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced): 36 weeks
  • Business English: 24 weeks
  • TOEFL/IELTS Preparation: 20 weeks
  • Conversational English Club: Ongoing membership
What is the class schedule?

We offer flexible scheduling with multiple time options:

  • Morning classes: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM CT
  • Evening classes: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM CT
  • Weekend options: Saturday morning intensive sessions
  • Most programs meet 3-4 times per week. Specific schedules are provided upon enrollment.

Enrollment & Payment

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrollment is easy! Simply:

  1. Take our free placement test (optional but recommended).
  2. Choose your course level and schedule preference from our course offerings.
  3. Complete the online application form for your chosen course.
  4. Make your payment or set up a payment plan as per the instructions you receive.
  5. Receive your course materials and login information for our student portal.
What are the course fees?

Our course fees vary by program:

  • Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced English: $1,299 per course
  • Business English: $1,399 per course
  • TOEFL/IELTS Preparation: $1,499 per course
  • Conversational English Club: $49 per month

We offer payment plans and early bird discounts. Please check individual course pages or contact us for current promotions!

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment options, including:

  • Full payment (often with a discount)
  • Installment plans (e.g., 3-month or 6-month plans)

Details are provided during the enrollment process for each course.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. Generally:

  • Full refund if you withdraw within the first week of the course (less any non-refundable registration fees).
  • Partial refunds may be available within the first month, depending on course progression.
  • Course credit for medical or emergency withdrawals may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Please refer to our Terms of Service for the detailed refund policy.

Technical Requirements

What equipment do I need for online classes?

You'll need:

  • A reliable computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access.
  • A webcam and microphone (these are often built-in).
  • Headphones or speakers for clear audio.
  • A stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps recommended for video conferencing).
  • An up-to-date web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
Which video platform do you use?

We primarily use Zoom for our live interactive classes. It's free to download and user-friendly. We also utilize a Learning Management System (LMS) where you can access course materials, assignments, and recordings of classes.

Are classes recorded?

Yes, most live class sessions are recorded and made available to enrolled students for a limited time (typically 30 days) after each session. This allows you to review material or catch up if you unavoidably miss a class.

Support & Contact

How can I contact student support?

You can reach our student support team via:

  • Phone: (915) 555-0101 (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 5 PM CT)
  • Email: support@inglesidiomatico.com
  • Live Chat: Available on our website during business hours.
  • Student Portal: Submit support tickets through your student account.
What if I need to miss a class?

If you need to miss a class, we recommend the following:

  • Notify your instructor in advance if possible.
  • Watch the recorded session, typically available within 24 hours.
  • Complete any missed assignments or activities.
  • Contact your instructor during office hours if you have questions or need to catch up.
Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes! Upon successful completion of any course (meeting attendance and academic requirements), you will receive:

  • A digital certificate of completion from Ingles Idiomatico English Institute.
  • A grade report detailing your skills assessment.
  • A letter of recommendation (upon request, for exemplary students).
