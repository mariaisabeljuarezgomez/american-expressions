Welcome to Your AEI Journey

This handbook is your essential guide to navigating your studies at Ingles Idiomatico English Institute. Here, you'll find comprehensive information about our core curriculum, particularly our acclaimed "Inglés Idiomático" series, which forms the backbone of your language acquisition journey with us.

Our carefully structured programs, combined with these exceptional materials and the dedicated support of our TESOL-certified instructors, have paved the way for countless success stories. Your placement into a specific "Inglés Idiomático" level is determined by your initial assessment, ensuring you start at the point most beneficial for your growth. We are excited to embark on this educational adventure with you!

Our Core Curriculum: The "Inglés Idiomático" Series

The "Inglés Idiomático" series, developed by experts in TESOL for Spanish-speaking learners, is the cornerstone of our general English programs. Each level is meticulously designed to build your skills progressively and effectively.

Inglés Idiomático - Level 1

Begin your journey by mastering the fundamental building blocks of English. This level focuses on essential vocabulary, basic grammar structures, and practical conversational skills for everyday situations.

Inglés Idiomático - Level 2

Expand your understanding and build fluency. Level 2 introduces more complex grammatical concepts, broadens your vocabulary, and enhances your ability to engage in more detailed conversations.

Inglés Idiomático - Level 3

Refine your skills towards mastery. Level 3 focuses on advanced grammar, nuanced vocabulary, idiomatic expressions, and sophisticated communication for academic and professional contexts.

Making the Most of Your "Inglés Idiomático" Textbook

Your "Inglés Idiomático" textbook is more than just a book; it's a key to unlocking your English potential. To maximize your learning:

  • Prepare for Each Class: Review upcoming lessons to familiarize yourself with new vocabulary and concepts.
  • Actively Participate: Use the material as a reference during interactive class activities and discussions.
  • Complete All Exercises: The practice activities are designed to reinforce what you learn in class.
  • Review Regularly: Revisit past units to solidify your understanding and retention of grammar and vocabulary.
  • Ask Questions: Your instructor is there to help clarify any doubts you have about the material.

Beyond the Textbook: Enhanced Learning

While the "Inglés Idiomático" series provides a strong foundation, your learning at Ingles Idiomatico English Institute is enhanced by a wealth of supplementary resources. These include:

  • Access to our interactive online learning platform with additional exercises, quizzes, and multimedia content.
  • Recordings of live classes for review and reinforcement.
  • Curated links to external practice websites, videos, and articles.
  • Opportunities for real-time conversation practice in our Conversational English Club.
  • Direct support and feedback from your instructors during class and office hours.

Institute Policies & Academic Integrity

This handbook also serves as a reference for important Ingles Idiomatico English Institute policies, including our code of conduct, attendance requirements, and guidelines for academic integrity. We encourage all students to familiarize themselves with these policies to ensure a positive and respectful learning environment for everyone.

For detailed information, please refer to our Institute Policies page and our Terms of Service.
