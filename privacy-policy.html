Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information at American Expressions Institute.

  • Our Mission & Vision
  • Institute Policies
  • Terms of Service
  • Privacy Policy
  • FAQ
Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. Introduction

American Expressions Institute ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, enroll in our courses, or use our services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you:

  • Enroll in courses: Name, email address, phone number, address, date of birth, education level, language proficiency
  • Contact us: Name, email address, phone number, message content
  • Take placement tests: Test responses, proficiency assessments, recommended course levels
  • Attend classes: Attendance records, participation data, assignment submissions, grades
  • Make payments: Billing information, payment method details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)

2.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Website usage patterns and navigation paths
  • Device information (mobile, tablet, computer)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 Educational Records

For enrolled students, we maintain:

  • Course enrollment and completion records
  • Attendance and participation tracking
  • Assignment submissions and grades
  • Progress reports and assessments
  • Certificates and transcripts
  • Communication records with instructors

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Educational Services

  • Providing English language courses and educational services
  • Assessing your English proficiency level
  • Tracking your progress and academic performance
  • Issuing certificates and transcripts
  • Communicating with you about your courses

3.2 Administrative Purposes

  • Processing enrollments and payments
  • Managing your student account
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending important notices and updates
  • Maintaining attendance and academic records

3.3 Communication and Marketing

  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Sending course information and updates
  • Providing educational newsletters and resources
  • Promoting relevant courses and services (with consent)
  • Conducting satisfaction surveys

3.4 Legal and Safety

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting our rights and property
  • Ensuring the safety and security of our platform
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • Video conferencing platforms (e.g., Zoom)
  • Learning management systems
  • Email communication services
  • Website hosting and technical support
  • Data analytics and website improvement

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal process
  • Government investigations
  • Compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Protection of our legal rights

4.3 Educational Partners

With your consent, we may share academic records with:

  • Universities or colleges for transfer credit evaluation
  • Employers for verification of language skills
  • Testing organizations for exam preparation support

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data backup and recovery systems

5.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Limited access to personal information
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Incident response procedures

5.3 Physical Safeguards

  • Secure facilities and equipment
  • Controlled access to data storage areas
  • Proper disposal of physical media

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Correction

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Update your contact information and preferences

6.2 Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your educational records
  • Transfer your data to another educational institution
  • Receive your information in a commonly used format

6.3 Marketing Communications

  • Opt-out of marketing emails at any time
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters and promotional content
  • Choose your communication preferences

6.4 Account Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account and personal information
  • Note: Educational records may be retained as required by law
  • Some data may be retained for legitimate business purposes

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

7.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant content and advertisements

7.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods based on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Until you request deletion or 7 years after account closure
  • Educational Records: 7 years after course completion (as required by educational regulations)
  • Payment Information: 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt-out or 3 years of inactivity
  • Website Analytics: 26 months from collection

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure adequate protection for international transfers through:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
  • Binding corporate rules

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

For students aged 13-17, we require parental consent for enrollment and data processing.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you of significant changes via email
  • Provide notice on our website for material changes

Your continued use of our services after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
