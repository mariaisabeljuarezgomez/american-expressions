Personalized English Tutoring

Tailored 1-on-1 sessions to meet your unique learning goals and accelerate your fluency.

Your English Goals, Your Personalized Path

At Ingles Idiomatico English Institute, we understand that every learner is unique. Our Customized Tutoring Sessions offer a highly personalized approach to English language learning, designed to fit your specific objectives, learning style, and schedule.

Whether you're looking to overcome specific grammar challenges, prepare for an important presentation, improve your pronunciation, or simply accelerate your overall fluency, our experienced TESOL-certified tutors are here to guide you every step of the way. We focus on your individual needs to ensure you make rapid and meaningful progress.

Benefits of 1-on-1 Customized Tutoring

Targeted Learning

Focus entirely on your specific weaknesses and areas you want to improve, from grammar and vocabulary to speaking confidence.

Personalized Attention

Receive undivided attention from your tutor, allowing for immediate feedback, tailored explanations, and a learning pace set by you.

Flexible Scheduling

Arrange sessions at times that are most convenient for your busy lifestyle, ensuring consistency in your learning.

Accelerated Progress

Address your specific needs directly to overcome learning plateaus and achieve your English goals faster than in a group setting.

Increased Confidence

Practice speaking in a supportive, low-pressure environment, helping you build the confidence to use English in real-world situations.

Customized Materials

Learning materials and activities are selected or created specifically for your goals, whether for academic, professional, or personal reasons.

Who Can Benefit from Customized Tutoring?

Our customized tutoring sessions are ideal for:

  • Students preparing for specific exams like TOEFL, IELTS, or Cambridge tests.
  • Professionals needing to improve their Business English for presentations, meetings, or negotiations.
  • Individuals looking to enhance conversational fluency and pronunciation.
  • Learners who prefer a one-on-one learning environment over group classes.
  • Anyone wanting to target specific grammar points or vocabulary sets.
  • Students who need flexible scheduling to fit language learning into a busy life.

How Our Customized Tutoring Works

1

Initial Consultation & Needs Assessment

We start with a free consultation to discuss your current English level, learning objectives, preferred learning style, and availability. This helps us match you with the perfect tutor and outline a preliminary plan.

2

Personalized Learning Plan

Based on the assessment, your tutor will create a customized learning plan with clear goals, specific focus areas, and tailored materials designed to help you achieve your objectives efficiently.

3

Flexible Session Scheduling

Schedule your 1-on-1 online sessions at times that work best for you. We offer flexible scheduling options, including evenings and weekends, to accommodate your commitments.

4

Interactive & Engaging Sessions

Participate in dynamic, interactive sessions focusing on your target areas. Receive immediate feedback, practice extensively, and build confidence with your dedicated tutor.

5

Continuous Progress Monitoring

Your tutor will regularly assess your progress, provide constructive feedback, and adjust the learning plan as needed to ensure you stay on track and motivated.

6

Achieve Your Goals

With consistent effort and personalized guidance, you'll achieve your English language goals, whether it's acing an exam, excelling at work, or communicating confidently in everyday life.
